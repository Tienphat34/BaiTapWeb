A smile is afacial expression formedprimarily by flexing the muscles at the sides ofthe mouth.It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
